About Us

Our mission is to raise musicians with skill and character.

Legacy Music Academy is a Christian music school built to serve students, churches, and communities through excellent, practical instruction and positive mentorship.

Meet your tutor: Michael Alexis

Michael Alexis began his musical journey at a young age, playing drums in church and developing a deep love for worship music. Over the years, he has been privileged to perform with notable artists and bands including Gates Praise, Marc Isaacs, Nataki Constantine, Samuel Medas, Samantha Johnson, Joshua Ali, Sean Daniel, Peter Regis, Vanessa Briggs, and Rene Fortune.

His journey is defined by dedication to faith, versatility, and a desire to inspire others through music — not just as a drummer, but as a minister of music.

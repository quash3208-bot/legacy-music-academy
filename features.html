Features

Everything you need to grow as a drummer

Our curriculum is practical and performance-driven. Each student receives level-appropriate material and coaching.

Learning material includes

A

Technique

  • Grip, rebound, control, and dynamics
  • Rudiments & stick control
  • Sound and tone on the kit
B

Grooves

  • Timing and pocket
  • Common worship & contemporary patterns
  • Variations and fills
C

Independence

  • Hand/foot coordination
  • Hi-hat control
  • Creative orchestration
D

Speed & Endurance

  • Speed-building exercises
  • Relaxation and stamina
  • Consistent tempo at higher BPM
E

Phrasing

  • Musical sentences, builds, and drops
  • Transitions and dynamics
  • Serving the song
F

Live music & tracks

  • Playing to click and backing tracks
  • Stage awareness and cues
  • Confidence for live ministry
